Team building at Ada Safari

Jul 29, 2022

The situation was brought to a boil - both by work on numerous projects and by the atmospheric conditions in Belgrade.

It was more than necessary to cool our heads.

That's why we headed to Ada Safari, a hidden gem within the Belgrade Sea - Ada Ciganlije.

After we all gathered and enjoyed breakfast, we split into teams and the games could begin!

The canoe ride was a real hit and many of us didn't stay dry after this game. However, we continued with the competition, still in good spirits.

We shot the target with a bow and arrow and an air rifle.

We jumped in the sacks, played Tic-Tac-Toe, and pulled the rope.

And we finally tallied up the points and declared the winning team, it didn't even matter anymore - because we all had an equally good time.

And we were all very hungry, so we looked forward to a rich lunch.

In the end, we parted with full bellies, but most importantly - full batteries for new victories.
